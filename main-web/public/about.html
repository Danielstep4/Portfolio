About me

Hello my name is, Daniel Stepanishin
I’m a self taught frontend developer

My first interaction with coding was when I was 13. I had a knee injury and I was at home for a whole three months. When I say interaction, I mean copy paste some code and then be proud of myself that I created something from nothing.

But at the age of 13 i had other plans. I was passionate about swimming, and as soon as I recovored from my injury I forgot all about coding and kept moving with my passion, to become a great swimmer and to beat swimmers like micheal phelps. But as you can see, I didn’t got there because of chronic injuries in the knee, but i never gave up until i had no choice.

Then at the age of 18 I got recruited by the army, and I became passtionate about something else. I wanted to be the best commandor, the leader of the platoon, to fix every problem I encountered. So to do that I worked hard, learned from the older and exprienced people at the company, and this time I succeded , the feeling was amazing. By the end of the service, I wanted a new challange a new passion to follow and conquer. I knew it was coding.

I worked a year as a lifeguard to save some money, to have free time to learn without any distraction.

And since the corona virus hit I saw an opportunity to learn coding without any distraction. I fell in love immediately, because it’s the most powerful tool to fix problems not just for myself, but for other people too. I love to learn and challange myself, and this is why I think I’m perfect for this job.
